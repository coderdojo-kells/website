CoderDojo Kells Autumn Season 2019 Registration

CoderDojo Kells is a programming and technology club in the heritage town of Kells in the heart of the Royal County. We are part of the wider CoderDojo network of programming clubs who aim to share a love of technology with young people.

Our Autumn Season runs from Saturday 5th October to Saturday 23rd November inclusive from 3:30pm to 5pm.

Please complete the registration form below to sign up and watch our Facebook and Twitter pages for latest news and annoucements.
